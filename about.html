The Empower Legacy.

Founded in 2020 by a small group of visionary professionals in Kampala, Empower Investment Club emerged from a simple observation: individually, we struggle to save, but collectively, we are an investment powerhouse.

What started as a Friday saving circle of 10 people has blossomed into a sophisticated investment vehicle with over 250 active members. We have funded over 100 small businesses and collectively invested in real estate and government securities.
